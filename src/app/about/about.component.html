About.

My career in the tech sector kicked off by working in business development at InsideSales.com for their sales software solution. Later on I worked as a QA Analyst with the property estimation software platform at Xactware before quickly transitioning into a full stack automation testing role. I briefly worked with ZampleBox to create a web automated testing suite from the ground up before finding myself back at Xactware to help build our next generation platform.

I am a self starter and I continue to learn coding best practices on my own time everyday. I currently live near Utah's Silicon Slopes with my lovely wife and young son.
